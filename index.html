Snippet triggers

Use short trigger phrases like /update to instantly expand full messages, templates, or notes.

Works system wide

Expand snippets in any application including Office, Browsers, Terminals, IDEs, and Remote Desktop sessions.

Organized workflows

Group snippets into folders and enable or disable them per workflow without deleting anything.

Local first storage

All snippets are stored locally on your machine. No cloud sync, no tracking, and no external services.

Works offline

QSnippet works fully offline. Your snippets are always available whether you are connected or not.

Lightweight by design

Fast startup, low resource usage, and no unnecessary background processes.

Open source

Fully open source and MIT licensed. Transparent, auditable, and built for long term use.

Designed for real workflows

QSnippet is built for day to day work where repetitive typing slows you down. Whether you're in IT, development, support, legal, or operations, QSnippet removes repetitive typing and keeps you focused.
